Simple but delicious pizza recipe

This pizza dish is perfect for a cosy tv night or invite friends or family over.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Chili tomato sauce
  • Salami
  • Greak cheese
  • Red unions
  • Red paprica
    • Mozarella or pizza cheese

Steps

  1. Start with buying ready made pizza dough.
  2. Put the chili tomato sauce on the pizza
  3. Cut the paprica and red union
  4. Lay out salami, greek cheese, along with the cut veggies
  5. Top of with mozarella or pizza cheese
  6. Put the pizza in the oven according to the pizza dough instructions. Enjoy :)

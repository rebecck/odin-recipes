Grilled cheese sandwich aka toast

The go to dish, quick, easy and really tasty

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Put butter on the toasts
  2. Put cheese on the sandwich
  3. Slice up tomato and lay out
  4. Put the sandwiches together and grill them
